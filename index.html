About Us

Established in August 2016 with private capital for the purpose of producing 3D model and project documentation.
It bases its work on maximum utilization of software in combination with a proactive role in each stage of a project.
Whether you're navigating the seas of marine engineering, naval architecture, or maritime research, TehnikCAD is your trusted partner for top-tier solutions.
Explore our world of intricate designs and meticulous craftsmanship, and let us elevate your maritime vision to new horizons.

Our Mission

Provide a reliable and dedicated relationship with the client at the start of the project.
Analyze risk factors and eliminate any possible hurdles.
With good planning, a defined schedule and budget, we are able to provide stable and flexible engineering packages to fit any need, from conversions to new builds in the yachting, cruise, fishing, and offshore industries.
Our dedicated engineers are problem-solving-oriented and will provide a possible solution for every situation. In combination with engineering, we provide on-site follow-up and supervision.
