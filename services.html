About Our Services

At Tehnikcad, we specialize in providing exceptional piping detail design services tailored to the maritime sector, offshore ventures, yachts, fishing, dredging, and various offshore projects.
With a team of experienced professionals and a commitment to excellence, we are here to be your trusted partner in the design and optimization of piping systems.
